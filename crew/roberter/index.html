Roberta Nkiruka Uti

Roberta Nkiruka Uti born September 5th 1976, is a Nigerian born American based Writer, Producer, Director, Make up artist, Production manager, props maker, set designer, script supervisor and occasionally an actress

She was born in Delta state of Nigeria and grew up partially between the popular cities of Warri and Lagos, in a bid to get into the popular Nollywood industry, Roberta had to tech herself the art of Make up (SFX), her first film MUA job was Bleeding Tree, which featured the great Ngozi Ezeonu and Queen Nwokoye, a Pressing forward production.

She made her mark in the industry with that one job due to the complexity of the creativity she presented, this led to many others including Tears of Ojuigo, the Lion of Uja, mission to the grave, evil egg, impossible mission to name a few



Roberta also wrote, produced and acted in a short film called Maya. In the US She partnered with Collins Johne and a third person to produce an upcoming movie title ‘Miracle in the spring’ which was also written by her

For her most recent job ‘Recompense’ she worked as the Makeup artist, Props Maker, Script supervisor, Production Manager as well as acted the role of Efua, a nosy annoying sister in law to Brigitte who had not seen her only brother Kwame
