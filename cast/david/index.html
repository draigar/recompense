David Vandy, An Actor, Writer and Director

David has been a stage actor, playwright and director for several years. Here in the USA he directed and acted in plays performed at the John F Kennedy Center for performing arts in Washington, DC, Smithsonian Museum for African American Arts and Culture, Lee Center for Performing Arts in Virginia and also in New York.

Also acted in Films shot in the USA like: High Fashion, Xyza, The Patriot, Dynamic Range and All Things Unholy. David is also a Multi Media International Broadcast Journalist with the Voice of America in Washington, DC.
