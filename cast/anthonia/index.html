• Producer | Model | Entrepreneur
    Anthonia Ijeoma Nwosu

Anthonia Ijeoma Nwosu is a producer, model, enterprenuer and actress from Nigeria.

From Mburubu in Nkanu East Area of Enugu State . Born in Lagos State. Did primary and secondary School in Lagos. ( Ejigbo model primary school and Ejigbo community school Ejigbo Lagos state) A graduate of Arts university of port Harcourt Rivers State.

Read Theatre Arts. Did Nysc in yola Adamawa State. Served with Nigerian Customs Yola in 2004. A Nollywood actress, producer, Model n Entrepreneur. Drew from my innate talent when I appeared in several roles on TV.

Gained recognition quickly after staring in my first movie " conspiracy 2 " with veteran actress Onyeka Nwenu. Late Obi Madubuogwu. Charles Okafor. In 2002. Another iconic role was in " family Battle" with Kanayo O. Kanayo. Eucharia Anunobi. Have also stared in several movies and soap operas as well as daily live shows on TV. Which gained me more fans and attentions on and off the screen. Currently working on movie projects in the United States of America.
