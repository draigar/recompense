• Manager | Producer | Actress
    Florence Onuma

Florence Onuma is a Manager, producer and actress from Nigeria.

Florence Onuma is a graduate of Theatre Arts of the University of Jos Plateau state Nigeria. She also holds a diploma in law from Benue Polytechnic Ugbokolo, Benue state Nigeria. In November 1996 she was hired as a pioneer staff of African Independent Television where she worked as the head of department make-up unit. African Independent Television grew to be one of African biggest broadcasting services.

After a brief stint with the Television Giant, Florence Onuma moved over to Nigeria's film industry, Nollywood. As an actor. There she honed her skills to become one of the finest A-List Thespians to emerge from the continent with over 300 movies to her name.



She moved to The United State of America and continued with what she loves to do best. Acting. In Sept 2020, she joined BSK Studios as the studio manager and producer, taking a step back from the screen to being behind the scene. Florence Onuma is passionate about telling African stories the African way using the African voice, to inspire the next generation of filmmakers. She believes that if you can dream it, you can achieve it.

when she is not interpreting a script, she loves to cook, take pictures, and read her Bible. Florence Onuma is a fun-loving and devout Christian. She is married and blessed with lovely children
