• Comedian | Singer | Actress
    Helen Paul

Helen Paul is a comedian, singer and actress from Nigeria.

For her stand-up comedy ,she is known also as Tatafo, a character birthed from her characterized voice range that makes her sound like a child. Her Tatafo persona is a witty, sometimes naughty child who addresses societal issues in a satirical manner, with this persona she has been featured in several Nigerian Television programs as well as Radio stations

She is also known as Alhaja Don Jasi, A lady who brings juicy gossips and information using the extreme Yoruba language and some of it’s deep slangs

In 2019 she got her Doctorate Degree in Theater Arts from the University of Lagos. Helen has worked and served as a freelance and full-time presenter at several media houses in Nigeria. These include Lagos Television (LTV 8), Continental Broadcasting Service (CBS) to name a few, she also worked in MNet’s Africa Magic, where she co-hosted a show called JARA



In July 2012 Paul released her debut album Welcome Party, which contains Afro-Pop songs such as "Boju Boju", "Vernacular", "Gbedu", "God Forbid", an Afro RnB song titled "Children of the World", and "Use Calculator", an enlightenment song about the menace of the HIV-AIDs epidemic. She subsequently released some singles, including "Take It Back". In 2018, she released the audio and visuals of her single titled "Never Knew", an inspiring song about her developmental years and career progress thus far.

Her most recent job is with BSK studios in Maryland USA, she featured in the BSK Movie Recompense, where she played the role of Dupe Babalola, a certified nurse who had fallen out of love from her Husband, she killed him with the hope of marrying her much younger boyfriend who unknown to her also had his own agenda

Helen Paul is a versatile actress and can switch into any role or character(s) as it comes, this ability is what gave birth to Helen360, her multifaceted characters in one word
