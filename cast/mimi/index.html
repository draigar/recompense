Mimi M'mah Sesay a Sierra Leonen/British Writer, Producer and actress

Mimi M'mah Sesay a Sierra Leonen/British Writer, Producer and actress who has starred in several movies over the years. Born September 3, 1983, Koidu Town, Kono, Sierra Leone. Attended Koidu Girls Secondary School(KGSS) & University of West London in United Kingdom where she studied Nursing.

Children: Hakim Jabbie-Sesay & Zaeedah Sesay Parents: Diamond Business Man & Philanthropists Alhaji Ibrahim Sorie Sesay & Haja Posseh Sesay.

Mimi Sesay was nominated for Best Actress in a Supporting Role in California and a Recognition Award for Performance Act at the United Artist Excellence Award in UK which she won both in 2018
